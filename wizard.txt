Wizard- Dempsy

Dempsy was born in a small town called Eggsburg, he was a young boy who was very intrested in learning new skills such as magic. It was the year 1899 time 3AM the orcs attacked your 
town killing the imperial wizard of the country as well as every living creature there. The town was in ruins, buildings turned into ash, a thick black fog lurkerd over Eggsburg with 
a scent of blood running through the town, the town has turned into an ghost town. Dempsy the last surviver of Eggsburg has set his course to avenge his town by killing every orc that 
comes past him.
